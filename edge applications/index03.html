RGBA colors

RGBA colors work similarly to RGB colors, however, they contain an additional parameter to indicate opacity. See the syntax: rgba(red, green, blue, alpha). The additional content corresponds to a numerical value defined between 0.0 to indicate full transparency to 1.0 to indicate full opacity.
