HSL colors.

The HSLA color definition works similarly to the HSL, however, there is the addition of the alpha property to determine the intensity of the transparency. The syntax used is hsla(hue, saturation, lightness, alpha).

hex colors.

The hexadecimal colors are represented by the shades RR (red) — red, GG (green) — green and BB (blue) — blue. Values ​​must be defined between 00 and FF and specified with the expression #RRGGBB.
